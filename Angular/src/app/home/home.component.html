Casino1024


Casino1024 was born thanks to three second year students who came out the CFGS "Web Application Development" in Murcia, Spain.

The concept of the project consists on emulating a Casino game. In this case, very similar to what we know as Bingo, but with different features to give it a bit of personality.

The game is purely made for fun and it is not intended to be turned into a business nor to promote gambling addiction.

Thank you so much for trying our website!
