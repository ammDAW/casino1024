Casino1024


Casino1024 was born thanks to three second year students who came out the CFGS "Web Application Development" in Murcia, Spain.

The concept of the project consists of emulating what a Casino game would be, very similar to what we know as Bingo, but with different features to give it a bit of personality.

The game is purely made for fun and is not intended to be turned into a business nor to promote gambling addiction.

You need to be loged to play the game. Thanks you so much to try our web.
